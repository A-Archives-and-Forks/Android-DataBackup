<i>DataBackup</i> is based on <a href='https://github.com/YAWAsau/backup_script' target='_blank' rel='nofollow'>backup_script</a>. It was made with the consent of its author.

<i>DataBackup</i> is intended to backup and restore the data of your apps. It works on Arm32, Arm64, x86 and x86_64 devices with Android 9 or higher.

<br><b>Features include:</b>

* <b>Support multi-user/dual backup:</b> Even backup/restore across partitions!
* <b>Cloud:<b> Fully supports Rclone, which can realize local mounting of network disks of various service providers.
* <b>100% Data Integrity:</b> All the data will be retained, and there is no need to log in again or download additional packages.
* <b>Full:<b> Split Apk, Arm32, Arm64, x86, x86_64, Android9+.
* <b>Fast:</b> Support tar lz4 zstd(default).
* <b>Easy:</b> Just a few simple steps!
